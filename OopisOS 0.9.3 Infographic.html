So, What IS This OopisOS Contraption, Anyway?

Listen up, space ace! OopisOS (currently at the "astonishing" v0.9.3 "Whoa Mama" edition) is your very own, pocket-sized (if your pockets are browser-sized) operating system simulation! It's got more blinking lights and confusing commands than a Vohaul flagship's laundry controls! We're talking a full-blown retro terminal, a file system that *mostly* remembers where you put things (thanks, IndexedDB, you digital packrat!), user accounts (so you can blame your co-pilot), session saving (for when you inevitably crash... the OS, not your ship... hopefully), and an editor so advanced, it even wraps words! Sometimes!

100%
Pure, Unadulterated Browser Magic!

That's right, no pesky server calls, no weird alien installations. Just you, your browser, and the terrifyingly vast potential of JavaScript!
